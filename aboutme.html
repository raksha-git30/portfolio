About Me

I’m a B.Tech student passionate about coding and solving real-world problems through technology. My journey into the world of development began with a deep curiosity and has grown into a commitment to continuous learning.

I’ve participated in national-level competitions like Smart India Hackathon (SIH) and Kirloskar Vasundhara, which have sharpened my teamwork and problem-solving skills.

Formerly, I served as the Design Head of the IEEE Student Chapter and I’m currently an active member of Nexus, a dynamic web development club where I collaborate and grow alongside talented peers.

I also love competing in hackathons and engaging with the tech community to stay inspired and push my limits.

Outside of coding, I'm a state-level volleyball player — sports keep me grounded and teach me discipline, strategy, and leadership.

I’m always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn, build, and contribute to meaningful projects.
